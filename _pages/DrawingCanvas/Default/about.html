Welcome to Pixel Pandemonium!

Pixel Pandemonium is an all-online version of Post-It Pandemonium, a hands-on, "unplugged" CS activity in which students learn how GIF images are represented on a computer and construct a large version of a GIF image, using Post-It's for pixels. You can read more about Post-It Pandemonium by following this link: . We have adapted this activity for an online audience, making it definitely a plugged-in unplugged activity.

There are directions below for teachers preparing to lead a class through this activity, and for students preparing to participate in the activity. But remember, students: when in doubt, pay attention to your teacher! Enjoy, -- JL Popyack, WM Mongan
